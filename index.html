INTRODUCTION

Many people think of sharks as savage monsters with terrible teeth ready to attack any swimmer who passes by. A few large sharks do attack humans, but most are harmless-unless you're a fish ! Sharks first began swimming in the worlds oceans 350 million years age , even before the dinosaurs appeared. There are now over 340 different species , in all sorts of strange and wonderful shapes and sizes.
